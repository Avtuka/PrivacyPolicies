1. Introduction

Welcome to Joker, a casual offline game designed for a fun and simple experience. Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy outlines what information we collect and how it is used.

2. Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII). There is no account creation, login, or personal data submission required to use the app.

3. Internet & Third-Party Services

The Joker app functions entirely offline. It does not connect to the internet or integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics platforms.

4. Local Data Usage

All gameplay data (such as player names or selections) is stored temporarily in-memory during your session. Once the app is closed, this data is automatically cleared.

5. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. However, since no personal data is collected, there is no risk of data misuse.

6. Data Security

We do not transmit or store any data on remote servers. All interactions remain on your local device and are not accessible to us or any third party.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be reflected within this document.
